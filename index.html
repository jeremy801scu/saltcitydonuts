DAILY MENU

Traditional Glazed

A Traditional Glazed donut is a classic and timeless treat that satisfies any sweet tooth. This donut is made with a soft, fluffy dough that's fried to golden perfection and then coated in a sweet, sugary glaze. With its simple yet irresistible flavor, the Traditional Glazed donut is perfect for any occasion and pairs well with a cup of hot coffee. Whether you're a donut lover or a first-time customer, the Traditional Glazed donut is sure to be a crowd-pleaser that will leave you craving for more.

Chocolate Glazed

A chocolate glazed donut is a delicious and decadent treat that combines the rich flavor of chocolate with the light and fluffy texture of a donut. This donut starts with a soft and airy dough that is fried to golden perfection and then coated in a smooth and shiny layer of chocolate glaze. The glaze adds a slightly sweet and indulgent touch to the already mouth-watering donut, making it a popular choice for anyone with a sweet tooth. Whether enjoyed with a hot cup of coffee or as a quick and satisfying snack on the go, a chocolate glazed donut is a classic favorite that is sure to please.

Strawberry Glazed

A Strawberry Glazed donut is a delicious pastry that combines the sweet and juicy flavor of ripe strawberries with the classic taste of a glazed donut. The dough is made with fresh ingredients, and the strawberry glaze is made with real strawberry puree, giving it a natural, fruity sweetness. The glaze has a beautiful pink hue, and it is thick and smooth, covering the entire donut evenly. The Strawberry Glazed donut is a perfect choice for those who want a fruity and indulgent treat that is both satisfying and refreshing. Whether you enjoy it as a morning snack or an afternoon treat, this donut is sure to please your taste buds and leave you feeling delighted.

Boston Cream

A Boston Cream Donut is a classic pastry that's beloved by many. It's a yeast-raised doughnut that's filled with creamy vanilla custard and topped with a luscious layer of chocolate ganache. The doughnut itself is light and fluffy, with a golden exterior that's been fried to perfection. The filling is smooth and velvety, and the chocolate ganache adds a rich, decadent touch that ties everything together. It's a perfect balance of sweetness and indulgence that's sure to satisfy any sweet tooth. Whether you're a longtime fan of Boston Cream Donuts or trying one for the first time, it's a treat that's hard to resist!
